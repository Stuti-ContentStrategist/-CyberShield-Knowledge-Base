# How to Install CyberShield Security Suite

**Article Summary:** Learn how to download, install, and activate CyberShield on your device.

***

### Before You Begin

Make sure your system meets the requirements:

* Windows 10 or higher / macOS 12 or higher
* 8 GB RAM and 2 GB free disk space
* Active internet connection

***

### Steps

1. Go to [https://cybershield.com/download](https://cybershield.com/download).
2. Select your operating system and click **Download Now**.
3. Run the downloaded installer (_CyberShield\_Setup.exe_ or _CyberShield.pkg_).
4. Follow the on-screen wizard to complete the installation.

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/CyberShield Setup Wizard.png" alt="CyberShield Setup Wizard showing installation progress with status bar and Back, Next, and Cancel buttons."><figcaption><p><em>Figure 1: Setup Wizard displaying installation progress.</em></p></figcaption></figure>

5. Launch **CyberShield** and click **Activate License**.
6. Enter your product key and verify activation.
