# Fixing CyberShield Activation Issues

**Article Summary:** Learn what to do if CyberShield fails to activate.

***

### Possible Causes

* No internet connection
* Invalid or expired product key
* Server maintenance or temporary outage

***

### Solutions

1. Verify that your device is connected to the internet.
2. Re-enter your product key and double-check for typos.
